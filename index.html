Location

Join us for RubyConf India 2024 at Hotel Clarks Amer, Jaipur. This residential conference includes both event access and accommodation at the venue for a seamless experience.

Accommodation

Each ticket includes accommodation at the venue:

  • Check-In: 28th November, 2:00 PM
  • Check-Out: 30th November, 10:00 AM

To extend your stay, please contact Hotel Clarks Amer directly.

Getting to Jaipur

By Air:

Jaipur International Airport (JAI) is approximately 4 km from the hotel, with taxis and ride-sharing options like Uber and Ola readily available. Travel time is around 15-20 minutes.

By Train:

Jaipur Junction Railway Station, about 10 km from the hotel, connects Jaipur with major cities. Taxis and auto-rickshaws are available, with a 30-minute drive to the hotel.

By Road:

Jaipur is accessible via NH8 from Delhi (about 5 hours) and NH21 from Agra (about 4 hours).

Passports and Visas

International Visitors: Most international attendees require a visa to enter India. Visitors from several countries may be eligible for an e-visa. For details and to apply online, please visit the Indian e-Visa Portal.

Documents Required: Ensure you carry a valid passport (with at least six months validity) and other identification documents while traveling.

Venue and City Information

Hotel Clarks Amer

Hotel Clarks Amer offers luxurious accommodation and conference facilities, including dining options, a rooftop restaurant, WiFi, a fitness center, a spa, and a pool. Attendees staying here can easily access the conference venue without additional travel arrangements.

The City of Jaipur

Jaipur, also known as the "Pink City," is the capital of Rajasthan. Famous for its vibrant culture and stunning architecture, Jaipur combines ancient charm with modern amenities. Popular landmarks include:

Amber Fort
City Palace
Hawa Mahal
Jantar Mantar

Jaipur is also known for its handicrafts, jewelry, and textiles, making it an excellent place for shopping.

Safety Tips
  • Stay alert and secure your belongings in crowded areas.
  • Use registered taxis, hotel shuttles, or ride-sharing apps like Uber or Ola for transportation.
  • Save emergency contact numbers (112) and embassy details.
Essential Information
Money

The currency in India is the Indian Rupee (₹). Major credit and debit cards are accepted in hotels, restaurants, and shops. ATMs are widely available in Jaipur, and currency exchange can be done at the airport or banks.

Internet Access

WiFi is available at the hotel and conference venue. Many cafes and restaurants in Jaipur also offer free WiFi. For mobile internet, purchase a prepaid SIM card at the airport or nearby mobile stores. Airtel, Jio, and Vodafone Idea offer affordable prepaid plans with good coverage.

Local Transportation
  • Auto-rickshaws – Negotiable rates, ideal for short trips.
  • Buses – Jaipur City Transport Services operates city buses. Use Google Maps for public transit routes.
  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Apps – Uber and Ola are widely available and convenient.
Dining & Local Cuisine

Jaipur is known for its rich culinary heritage. Must-try dishes include dal baati churma, laal maas, and ghevar. Here are a few popular dining spots:

  • Chokhi Dhani – A cultural village resort offering traditional Rajasthani cuisine.
  • Handi Restaurant – Known for flavorful curries and tandoori dishes.
  • Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar (LMB) – Famous for sweets and vegetarian Rajasthani food.
Things to Do in Jaipur

Beyond the conference, Jaipur offers plenty to explore:

  • Visit the Amber Fort – One of the most majestic forts in India.
  • Explore the City Palace – A stunning blend of Mughal and Rajput architecture.
  • Shop at Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar – Famous for jewelry, textiles, and handicrafts.
  • Attend a cultural show – Enjoy Rajasthani dance and music performances.
